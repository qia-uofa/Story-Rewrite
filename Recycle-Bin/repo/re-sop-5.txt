Harry spends his last month with the Dursleys mostly ignored by them, while he prepares excitedly for Hogwarts with his owl, Hedwig. On September first, Uncle Vernon takes him to King's Cross but mocks him when there appears to be no Platform Nine and Three-Quarters. Confused and nearly panicking, Harry overhears a red-haired wizarding family discussing the same platform.

The mother, Mrs. Weasley, kindly explains that Harry must walk straight through the barrier between platforms nine and ten. Harry follows her advice and successfully reaches Platform Nine and Three-Quarters, where the Hogwarts Express is waiting. He finds an empty compartment and gets help from Fred and George Weasley loading his trunk.

The Weasley twins recognize Harry by his lightning-shaped scar, and Harry overhears the family talking about him with curiosity and sympathy. As the train departs, Mrs. Weasley and Ginny wave goodbye, and Harry begins his journey to Hogwarts.
