Read all sub-summary files from `<env:SOURCE_MASK>/` in order — named `000.md`, `001.md`, `002.md`, etc.

Build a comprehensive **Lorebook** and write it to `./lorebook.md`.

## Required sections

### Characters
For each named character: full name, role, physical description (if mentioned), personality traits, relationships, and arc across the story.

### Locations
For each distinct setting: name, description, atmosphere, and the significant events that occur there.

### Timeline of Key Events
Numbered chronological list of the most plot-significant events, noting which part they occur in (Part 000, 001, …).

### Relationships
Important relationships between characters — alliances, rivalries, romances, family bonds, betrayals.

### Objects & Artifacts
Significant items, weapons, heirlooms, or MacGuffins, with their role and significance.

### Themes & Motifs
Recurring themes, symbols, and motifs, with examples of where they appear.

## Quality bar

The lorebook is the **canonical reference** for the entire rewrite. Thorough, internally consistent, and precise. Every claim grounded in the sub-summaries.

## Rules

- Write **only** to `./lorebook.md`
