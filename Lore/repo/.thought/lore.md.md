## Goal
Create a lore book at `./lore.md` that introduces the world, major characters, and timeline based on the provided chapter summaries.

## Design Decisions
- Structure the lore book into clear sections: premise, worldbuilding, institutions, magic, factions, characters, artifacts, locations, themes, and timeline.
- Focus on the events and details present in the source summaries, covering Harry’s early life, the Dursleys, the wizarding world’s hidden society, Hogwarts, Diagon Alley, Gringotts, wands, and the journey to school.
- Use an accessible reference-guide tone rather than chapter-by-chapter prose.
- Include enough context for a reader to understand the setting and stakes without reproducing original text.

## Potential Pitfalls
- Avoid overextending beyond the supplied summaries; only include lore clearly supported by them.
- Avoid copying distinctive prose from the source material.
- Make the timeline coherent even though the supplied material ends during the train journey before arrival at Hogwarts.
