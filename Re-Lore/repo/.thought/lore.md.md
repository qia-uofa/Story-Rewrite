## Goal
Rewrite the provided lore-book while preserving its reference-document structure and original plot-line functions.

## Design Decisions
- Map the original character roles onto characters from *The Boys*: Hughie as the protected famous survivor, Homelander as the feared vanished enemy, Butcher as the rough guide, Mallory as the distant strategist, Edgar/Stillwell/A-Train as the repressive household, and other Boys characters as school peers or institutional figures.
- Replace magic with *Invincible*-style superhero science: alien biology, Viltrumite conflict, GDA secrecy, enhanced physiology, neural weapons, hidden transit systems, public hero culture, and cosmic defense institutions.
- Preserve each original event in adapted form: childhood suppression, strange incidents, letters, remote escape, birthday revelation, hidden marketplace, vault retrieval, bonded tool, return home, secret platform, and academy train journey.

## Potential Pitfalls
- The world should feel like *Invincible* without importing too many *Invincible* characters as character replacements, since the prompt requires *The Boys* characters.
- The plot-line must remain recognizable while replacing all magical assumptions.
- Cross-references must stay consistent across character entries, institutions, terminology, and timeline.
