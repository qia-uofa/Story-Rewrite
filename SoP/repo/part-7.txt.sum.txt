Hermione is surprised that Harry does not already know more about Hogwarts houses and says she hopes to be sorted into Gryffindor, though Ravenclaw would not be too bad. She leaves with Neville to continue looking for his toad, reminding Harry and Ron to change because they will arrive soon. Ron, annoyed by Hermione and by a failed spell from George, says he hopes she is not in his house. He explains that his family has all been in Gryffindor and worries about disappointing them, especially if he were sorted into Slytherin.
