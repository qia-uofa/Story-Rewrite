Harry spends his final days before Hogwarts isolated at the Dursleys’ house, reading his schoolbooks and counting down to September first with his owl, Hedwig. The Dursleys reluctantly take him to King’s Cross, where Uncle Vernon mocks the idea of Platform Nine and Three-Quarters and abandons him between platforms nine and ten.

Confused and close to panic, Harry notices a red-haired wizarding family discussing Muggles and follows them. Mrs. Weasley kindly explains how to reach the hidden platform: he must walk straight through the barrier. Harry does so and finds the Hogwarts Express waiting.

On the platform, Harry sees students, families, pets, and trunks everywhere. He boards the train with help from Fred and George Weasley, who recognize him by his lightning-shaped scar. From his compartment, Harry watches the Weasley family say goodbye. He learns that Percy is a prefect, Ron is starting at Hogwarts too, and Ginny is excited to see him. As the train leaves, the Weasleys wave goodbye and Harry begins his journey to Hogwarts.
