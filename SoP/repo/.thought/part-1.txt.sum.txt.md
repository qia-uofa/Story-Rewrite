## Goal
Create a concise summary of `part-1.txt`, which contains the opening chapter of a novel.

## Design Decisions
- Summarize the main events in original wording rather than reproducing the source text.
- Include the key setup: the Dursleys’ ordinary life, strange magical events, Voldemort’s fall, the deaths of Harry’s parents, and Harry being left with his aunt and uncle.
- Keep the summary clear and compact while preserving the chapter’s narrative arc.

## Potential Pitfalls
- Avoid quoting extended passages from the copyrighted source.
- Do not include unnecessary detail that would make the summary too long.
